#
# Lines starting with # are stripped before sending to the model.

Style preferences for meeting summaries:
- Write in a professional but approachable tone.
- Use bullet points for lists, not numbered lists.
- Avoid em-dashes, horizontal lines, icons, or emoji.
- Keep section headings short and descriptive.
- Prefer specific names and details over vague references.
- When quoting or paraphrasing, attribute to the speaker.
- For action items, always include the owner if known.
